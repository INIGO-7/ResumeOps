# 2 — Academic background

**What lives here.** Every formal qualification: degrees, institutions, cities, start and end dates, grade or GPA and its scale, honours and distinctions, thesis or final-project title with a one-line description of what was actually built or proven, relevant coursework, exchange or visiting periods, and bootcamps or intensive programmes with enough substance to be defended in an interview.

**What does not live here.** Certifications, licences and badges — those go in `7-certifications-and-training.md`. Academic papers go in `8-publications-and-talks.md`.  Academic prizes go in `9-awards-and-recognition.md`, with a pointer back to the degree here.

**How to record it.** One `###` heading per qualification, most recent first, formatted `Degree — Institution (City, Country), YYYY-MM – YYYY-MM`. Under it, `- **Field:** value` lines for grade, thesis, coursework and anything else. Keep the thesis description concrete: what the work did, not what the field is about. If a date, grade or title is unknown, write `TODO — ask candidate` rather than guessing.
