# 7 — Certifications and training

**What lives here.** Credentials issued by a body other than a university: professional certifications, cloud and vendor certificates, licences, badges, language certificates with their level and scale, and substantial courses or training programmes worth naming.  Per item: exact official title, issuer, date obtained, expiry or renewal date, credential ID or verification URL, and one line on what it actually covered.

**What does not live here.** Degrees and bootcamps go in `2-academic-background.md`.  Spoken language *fluency* is recorded in `1-identity-and-constraints.md`; the certificate that proves it is recorded here.

**How to record it.** One line per credential:

`- **Official title** — Issuer, YYYY-MM (expires YYYY-MM) · ID/URL · one line on scope`

Use the issuer's exact naming, since ATS keyword matching is literal. Flag expired credentials as `EXPIRED` rather than deleting them, and record in-progress ones with a target date so they can be listed as such.
