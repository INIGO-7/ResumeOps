# 3 — Work experience

**What lives here.** The professional record, one entry per role held: employer, what the employer does and its size, exact job title (plus any internal title that differs), city and work arrangement, start and end dates, whether it was full-time, contract, internship or freelance, team size and reporting line, the scope owned, the technologies and tools used day to day, and a plain description of the work — systems built, problems handled, who the users were, what changed while the candidate was there. Include promotions and internal moves as separate sub-entries under the same employer.

**What does not live here.** The quantified outcomes of that work belong in `4-achievement-bank.md`, which points back to the role recorded here. Unpaid side projects and client work done outside a job go in `6-projects.md`.

**How to record it.** One `##` heading per employer, most recent first, then a `###` heading per role at that employer formatted `Title, YYYY-MM – YYYY-MM (or Present)`.  Under each role: `- **Field:** value` lines for arrangement, team, stack and scope, then a short prose paragraph or bullet list of what the work actually was. Write it long and specific — this is a store, not a CV; selection and compression happen at drafting time.  Unknown dates or titles get `TODO — ask candidate`.
