# 9 — Awards and recognition

**What lives here.** Third-party validation: awards and prizes, competition placements, scholarships and grants, promotions and their stated justification, performance review ratings and excerpts, 360 or peer feedback, recommendation letters, client or manager testimonials, and verbatim praise worth quoting. Per item: what it was, who granted it, when, the selection pool or how competitive it was, and — for feedback — a direct quote with its author's role.

Verbatim quotes matter: they are the raw material for a summary line, and they are the strongest evidence a recruiter can be pointed at.

**What does not live here.** The work that earned the recognition belongs in `3-work-experience.md` or `6-projects.md`, and its measurable outcome in `4-achievement-bank.md`. This file records the recognition itself.

**How to record it.** Two `##` groups.

- **Awards** — one line each: `- **Award name** — granting body, YYYY · selection pool · what it was for`
- **Feedback and testimonials** — one `###` per source, with the quote in a blockquote and a line naming the author's role, relationship to the candidate, and date. Mark whether the quote may be reproduced publicly.
